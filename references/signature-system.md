# Signature system

Shape a showcase as an evidence-led hero, followed by a lighter explanatory material field, a darker proof or demonstration surface, and a quiet closing. The visual weight should move with the argument: establish the claim, explain it, substantiate it, then leave space for the next action.

Each section gets one dominant headline and a single action or attention colour. Do not introduce competing accent colours merely to vary the composition. Media and data windows must be opaque enough to remain readable over the continuous background.

Use `power3.out` or `power4.out` easing for entrances and state changes. Reject bounce, elastic easing, perpetual decorative motion, and scroll hijacking. Motion should be brief, intentional, and subordinate to the evidence.
