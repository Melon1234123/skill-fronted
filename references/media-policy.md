# Media policy

Supplied or properly licensed media comes first. Generated imagery must be disclosed wherever it is used and can never serve as proof of a claim, result, or capability.

When no appropriate media exists, build the page with CSS and typography rather than filling it with random images. Use generated or illustrative material only when its role is clear and non-evidentiary.

Keep heavy video out of normal source commits. Store it through the approved delivery path, provide a lightweight fallback, and make sure the page remains understandable before the media loads or when it cannot play.
