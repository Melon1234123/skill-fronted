# Adaptive art direction

Before code, prepare exactly **2–3** distinct art directions for the user to compare. Every direction must include:

- a name;
- audience and action fit;
- palette;
- material or texture;
- the role of real, generated, and fallback media;
- typography;
- section and motion rhythm; and
- accessibility and performance risk.

Require an explicit user selection before implementation. Do not copy roads, autonomy terms, terrain contours, or LiDAR from the inspected source. The direction must translate the approved design principles into an original visual identity rather than reproducing a domain-specific look.
